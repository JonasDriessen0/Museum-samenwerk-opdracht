Welcome to the National Video Game Museum!

Here you can discover and play 250+ games from retro to modern games. We are proud to have one of the largest collections of games in Europe. Whether you are a passionate gamer or simply interested in the history of the gaming industry, we offer something for everyone. Visit our museum and make memories you won't soon forget. Thank you for visiting our website, we hope to see you soon in our museum!
